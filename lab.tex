The Fox and the Crab

    A Crab one day grew disgusted with the sands in which he lived. He decided to take a stroll to 
the meadow not far inland. There he would find better fare than briny water and sand mites. So off 
he crawled to the meadow. But there a hungry Fox spied him, and in a twinkling, ate him up, both 
shell and claw.

        "Be content with your lot."
